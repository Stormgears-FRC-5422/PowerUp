moveStraight

suspend fun moveStraight(distance: Double, theta: Double): Unit

The intended purpose of this method is to move the robot at a given angle (theta) for a given distance (distance). The distance should be given in inches, and the theta in radians. The method will convert the distance into encoder ticks in order to facilitate motion magic. Ensure that when calling this method.

Parameters

distance -

  • the distance to move the robot in inches

theta -

  • the angle at which to move the robot
