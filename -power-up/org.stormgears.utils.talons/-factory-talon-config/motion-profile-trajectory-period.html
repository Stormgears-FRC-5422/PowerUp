motionProfileTrajectoryPeriod

open val motionProfileTrajectoryPeriod: Int

Overrides TalonConfig.motionProfileTrajectoryPeriod

When trajectory points are processed in the motion profile executer, the MPE determines how long to apply the active trajectory point by summing baseTrajDurationMs with the timeDur of the trajectory point (see com.ctre.phoenix.motion.TrajectoryPoint). This allows general selection of the execution rate of the points with 1ms resolution, while allowing some degree of change from point to point.
